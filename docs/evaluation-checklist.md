# Level 3 Evaluation Checklist

This document defines how to evaluate the AI feedback quality for the Mid Laning Decision Review AI.

The goal is not to check whether the AI gives one perfect answer.
The goal is to check whether the AI gives useful, grounded, situation-aware coaching feedback.

---

# 1. Evaluation Goal

Each generated review should be evaluated based on these questions:

1. Did the AI understand the player's situation correctly?
2. Did the AI focus on the correct outcome?
3. Did the AI identify the correct coaching categories?
4. Did the AI avoid overconfident claims?
5. Did the AI give specific and practical feedback?
6. Did the AI give one clear next-game action?
7. Did the AI avoid making up information that was not provided?

---

# 2. Score Table

Use a 1 to 5 score for each category.

```text
1 = Poor
2 = Weak
3 = Acceptable
4 = Good
5 = Excellent
